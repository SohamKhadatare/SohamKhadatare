4th Poster Typography Page
4th Poster Typography Page
Graphic Design
A submission for the competition conducted by The Museum of Typography, Crete, Greece, every year to celebrate Typography.
The idea was to develop a design to show how the designs of the past, present and future were supported on the pillars of typography.
The Colour Theme
The colours were shades of pink and white as they brought out and expressed the love for type
The Typeface
The fonts were chosen in such a way so as to represent each era of font styles in the poster. 'Pirata One' for the olden gothic times, 'Montserrat' for the clean modern times and 'Mission Gothic' for the futuristic geometric sans feel.
