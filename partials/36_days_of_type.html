36 Days of Type
Illustration
36 Days of Type is a project that invites Designers, Illustrators and Graphic Artists to express their particular view on letters and numbers of our alphabet.
My idea was to create a set of illustrations that resonate with the letter of the day. The illustrations were based on an activity or profession that makes our lives more livier and joyful.
The alphabet is made from the font, Helvetica (Bold) as it gives sufficient place to play with the negative space without loosing the form of the alphabet. Rest of the text is in ITC Avant Garde with variable weights. The Illustrations have a colour theme. In each of the illustrations, there is a priary colour, shades of which are used for different parts. I've used 2-3 secondary colours based on the necessity to bring in a contrast and highlight the activity being depicted.
