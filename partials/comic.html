Kazakh Zhiza Stories
Kazakh Zhiza Stories
Comic Book
The colection of comics in this book serve as a travelogue of my trip to Kazakhstan as an exchange student in the summer of 2018. Within it are some of the instances from the trip that stand out due to special reasons. The characters in this book are not fictional at all and resemble real people.
The effort in making this book was immense but it would have been impossible to complete this endeavour without the guidance of Prof. Phani Tetali from the IDC Department of IIT Bombay. I would also like to thank all the participants and volunteers of AUA Deep Dive program held in Kazakhstan in the summer of 2018.
