Token, the Toucan
Token, the Toucan
Mascot Design
Mood Indigo, the Cultural Fest of IIT Bombay is envisioned to bring people from different backgrounds together and celebrate the cultural diversity of the country. For the mascot, a bird or animal was needed that represented this diversity and brought out the liveliness of the festival. Toucan was chosen as it represents the diversity with the diverse colour palate and its vibrancy brings out the mood of the festival.
The Ideology
The goal of choosing a mascot was to find an entity that represents the cultural fest as a whole. Toucans, being vibrant, colourful and social birds, were chosen to be the mascot. Token is wearing a t-shirt to make him more human like and hence feel as a comfortable friendly companion and build a human connect with the people. Highlights and shadows have been used to give depth to the character. The mascot has been named as Token, because he is a token of good luck and it rhymes with the name of the bird it has been designed after.
The Colours
Vibrant colours have been used to make Token livelier and fun. Colours have been used in such a way so as to not deviate from the actual look of a toucan. Orange, Blue, Navy Blue and Yellow have been used to retain the original feel of the toucan. Green shirt has been chosen to diversify the colour palate.
The Typeface
Fonts chosen for the design are Dopestyle, Panton and Gotham font family. These fonts give a clean look to the design. Panton was used because it is bold, clean and eye catching; perfect for titles and headers. Gotham was used because it is one of the few clean sans serif fonts that do not affect readability of large volume of text.
Use Cases
The Design can be incorporated in the whole branding process. For example, merchandise like booklets, pens, apps, visiting cards can incorporate the mascot to enhance the quality of the festival. The mascot can be used for the t-shirts used by the organizers and coordinators of the cultural festival.
