Culturals Website
Culturals Website
UI/UX Design
Culturals is a student body of IIT Bombay that caters to the students interested in pursuing a hobby which is not related to the academic curiculum like design, dance, dramatics, fine arts, photography, film-making,etc. The essence of the work dont by the body can be summarized by their slogan - “To Life. To Surprises. To Discovery”
The Brief
The original Culturals Webpage did not get enough activity due to probelms with poor UI and UX of the site. It was important to increase activity as the page has many microsites and portals which help in managing services.
The Approach
The solution involved revamping the Ui and UX to reduce the number of screens and to reduce the navigational effort through the contents of the website.
